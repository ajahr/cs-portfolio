The Basics

My name is Aja, aka macaronimuchacha. I am a high school student that attends a charter school in California, where I take a UC approved Intro to Computer Science Course. This class teaches the basics of CSS, HTML, and some JavaScript. Slowly but surely, I found myself becoming invested in webpage design and making small websites that looked nice.

If you're wondering about the name, I really like macaroni and am a young girl, so chose to represent myself with "macaroni muchacha." It also matches my friend, Kayla, aka mochimuchacha .

This is the home page of a website that's about me and my endeavors. You can click the links at the top in the nav bar to explore.

General Skills I've Learned

Through this class, I learned the imprortance of perseverance, research, and precision. While an issue might seem impossible to solve at first, I had to research and keep trying until I cracked it. The other incredibly important part of coding was precision. One tiny mistake like forgetting to close one tag would throw off the entire project.

Computer Science Exploration

The computer science class I'm taking is a UC approved course that introduces concepts like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. At the beginning, I was hesitant to take this course, because I had been introduced to code before and didn't like it. I also had far fewer skills, as shown by the first website I ever made on the left.

In just one week, I've learned how to

  • Make headers and change their size and colors
  • Add paragraphs
  • Add navbars to pages
  • Add backgrounds
  • Use "div" commands to format pages
  • Use colors and fonts
  • Add images
  • And much more!
